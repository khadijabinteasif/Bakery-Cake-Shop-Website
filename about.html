_Our Purpose

About Kakes

Founded in Multan in 2024, the philosophy of Kakes Bakeshop was simple; to create connections through desserts while striking the ideal balance between flavor and feeling.

Since the beginning, Kakes has secured an irreplaceable spot in the hearts of people.

Being the quintessential representation of aesthetics and taste, a Kakes dessert is consumed by the eyes well before delighting the tastebuds!

_Reaching Nationwide

Growing & Expanding

The love for Kakes has only blossomed with time. We now have multiple stores in different locations including Multan and Lahore and this is just the beginning.

Our dream to bake the best desserts in town continues to flourish, and we are over the moon to have created a sweet utopia for dessert lovers to make their sweet escape from reality to fantasy!
